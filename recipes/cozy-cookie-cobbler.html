Cozy Cookie Cobbler

Description:

This 4-ingredient apple cookie cobbler is the easiest version of fruit cobbler ever made.
Apple pie filling, topped with sugar cookie dough and dusted with cinnamon sugar, bakes
into a delicious treat. Try it with any fruit pie filling.

🥚 ingredients:

  • 2 cans pie filling, such as apple, blueberry
    or cherry
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1 tube refrigerated sugar cookie dough

📝 Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees C and lightly grease the sides of
    a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  2. Pour pie filling into the bottom of the dish. Break apart cookie dough into 10 to
    12 smaller pieces and flatten them very slightly. Place them randomly over the
    top of the pie filling, covering as much of the top as possible, leaving some parts
    of pie filling uncovered.
  3. Stir together sugar and cinnamon in a small bowl and sprinkle mixture evenly
    over the top of cookie dough.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until lightly browned and bubbly, for 30 to 35
    minutes. Serve with vanilla ice cream if desired.
