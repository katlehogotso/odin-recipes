Dubai Chocolate Cake

Description:

This 4-ingredient Dubai Chocolate Cake is the easiest version ever.
A rich and moist pistachio cake topped with whipped chocolate frosting
and crunchy threads of toasted kataifi.

🥚 ingredients:

  • 355ml lemon-lime soda, such as Sprite
  • 1 box pistachio cake mix
  • 1 container chocolate frosting
  • 1 cup toasted shredded phyllo dough (kataifi)

📝 Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees C. Grease a 9x13-inch cake pan.
  2. Whisk Sprite and cake mix together in a bowl until smooth. Pour cake batter into
    the prepared pan.
  3. Bake cake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted in the center comes
    to 25 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile, beat chocolate frosting with an electric mixer to increase volume.
    Toast kataifi in a skillet over medium heat until lightly golden, 3 to 5 minutes.
  5. Allow cake to cool completely, about 30 minutes. Frost with whipped chocolate
    frosting, and top with toasted kataifi.
