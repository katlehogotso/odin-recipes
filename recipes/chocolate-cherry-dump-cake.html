Chocolate Cherry Dump Cake

Description:

This 3-ingredient chocolate cherry dump cake could not be easier!
Just pie filling, chocolate cake mix, and butter — you will have it
ready to bake before you can say, preheated oven.

ingredients:

  • 2 cans cherry pie filling
  • 1 box chocolate cake mix
  • 1/2 cup butter, cut into 16 slices

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Pour pie filling into a 9x13-
    inch baking dish.
    Stir 1/4 teaspoon almond extract into the pie filling for extra flavor if you
    like.
  2. Spread cake mix evenly over the pie filling, taking care not to mix it into the
    fruit. Top evenly with butter slices.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until lightly golden brown and bubbly, about 45
    minutes. Let stand at least 5 minutes before serving.
