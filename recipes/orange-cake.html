Orange Cake

Description:

This 2-ingredient orange cake will be the easiest cake you will ever bake.
Made in a 9x13-inch pan, you can frost, decorate, or top it with anything you
like for any occasion.

🥚 ingredients:

  • 1 package vanilla cake mix
  • 1 can orange flavored soda

📝 Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 175 degrees C. Grease a 9x13-inch baking
    dish.
  2. Combine cake mix and soda in a large bowl. Use a hand whisk to mix ingredients
    until light and smooth, about 3 minutes. Pour batter into a prepared baking
    dish.
  3. Bake cake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted in the center comes
    out clean, 35 to 40 minutes. Allow cake to cool before frosting, if desired, and
    cutting. Serve each slice with 1 tablespoon of Cool Whip, if desired.
