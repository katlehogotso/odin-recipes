Cozy Cookie Cobbler

Description:

This 4-ingredient apple cookie cobbler is the easiest version of fruit cobbler ever made.
Apple pie filling, topped with sugar cookie dough and dusted with cinnamon sugar, bakes
into a delicious treat. Try it with any fruit pie filling.

ingredients:

  • 2 cans pie filling, such as apple, blueberry
    or cherry
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1 tube refrigerated sugar cookie dough
